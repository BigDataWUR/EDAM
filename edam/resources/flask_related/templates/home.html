Wageningen INF Group

Our mission is to contribute to the state-of-the-art of smart systems and system of systems engineering to support innovations in the life sciences application domains. Hereby, we focus on software engineering, knowledge engineering and socio-technical systems engineering.

ecologismico

Ecologismico is a neologism from the Greek words ecology and logismico (the modern Greek word for software), that glues together logos (stands for reasoning in ancient Greek) to define the study of natural systems language and the research of intelligent software systems for the environment. Software Engineering for the Environment is a research group (aka "collaboratory") of the Internetworked Systems Lab of the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Democritus University of Thrace.
